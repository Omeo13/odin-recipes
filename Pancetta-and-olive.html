Pancetta and Olive pasta

This delicious pasta dish combines the savory flavors of pancetta and olives, creating a perfect balance of taste and texture. It's quick to prepare and makes for a satisfying meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 100g pancetta, diced
  • 50g black olives, pitted and sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  5. Add the olives and cooked pasta to the skillet. Toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, add red pepper flakes for a bit of heat.
  • Use fresh pasta for a more authentic taste.
  • Pair with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
  • Store leftovers in the fridge for up to 2 days.
