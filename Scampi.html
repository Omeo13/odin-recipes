Shrimp and Linguini Scampi

Shrimp and Linguini Scampi is a classic Italian-American dish that features succulent shrimp sautéed in a garlic butter sauce, served over al dente linguini pasta. The dish is known for its rich flavors and is often garnished with fresh parsley and lemon juice for added brightness. It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 200g linguini pasta
  • 300g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Cook the linguini according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. If using, add the white wine and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Toss in the cooked linguini and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, add red pepper flakes for a bit of heat.
  • Use fresh shrimp for the best taste and texture.
  • Pair with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
  • Store leftovers in the fridge for up to 2 days.
