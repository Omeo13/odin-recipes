Bologenese

Bolognese sauce, or rag√π alla Bolognese, is a traditional Italian meat-based sauce originating from Bologna. It's typically served with pasta, such as tagliatelle, and is known for its rich flavor and hearty texture.The sauce is made with ground meat, tomatoes, onions, carrots, and a variety of herbs and spices. It's a comforting dish that's perfect for family meals or special occasions. The key to a great Bolognese is to simmer it slowly, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully.

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef
  • 100g ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes, crushed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and carrot, cooking until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Add the ground beef and pork, cooking until browned.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve over pasta, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce.
  • Let the sauce simmer longer for deeper flavors.
  • Pair with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
  • Store leftovers in the fridge for up to 3 days or freeze for later use.
